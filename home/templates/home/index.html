About

FreeFido is a dog park with a difference, acres of nature just for you and your dogs. At our private dog park, you'll find various amenities designed to enhance your visit. We provide shaded areas and water stations throughout the park, ensuring your dogs stay hydrated and comfortable even on warm days. You can relax and unwind in designated seating areas (with a yummy coffee from our coffee hut!) and allow your dog to run and play freely, promoting their physical fitness and overall well-being. Come and visit us just outside of Mullingar, Co.Westmeath.

Safety

At Freefido, safety is of utmost importance to us. As you build a connection with your dog, positive interaction is key. In our private, fully-fenced dog park, you can book a time that is yours alone knowing that there will be no escapes or concerns as our parks entrance is fully monitored. Additionally, we only allow registered members and their dogs inside the park, guaranteeing a controlled and safe environment. Join us today and make a booking!

  • Fully enclosed 6ft fence
  • Secure entrance/exit
  • No toxic plants or trees
  • Time booked is yours alone
  • Perfect for recall training
  • Park inspected twice daily

Benefits

Come and let your dog run and sniff in a safe environment that benefits both their mental and physical health. Dogs need to sniff and we guarantee that our park will provide them with all of the stimulation that they need. Bring some treats, or pick some up from our coffee hut, and work on your dogs recall. Spend time with your dog off leash and strengthen your bond with some playtime. Check out our articles section and discover some new tips or share your training wisdom with other park users.

  • Let your dog do what they do best
  • Decrease reactivity
  • Improve recall skills safely
  • Keep your dog at a healthy weight
  • Trim nails naturally
  • Strengthen your bond
